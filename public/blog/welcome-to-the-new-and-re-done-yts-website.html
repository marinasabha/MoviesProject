    First of all, welcome to our new website. We hope you love it as much as we do.

    Addition of the Blog

    Combining all the torrent qualities to one movie

    This has been one of the most requested changes over the past 2-3 years. Until now it was impossible to achieve due to our initial database structure, but it has all been reworked now. We have now combined all the torrent qualities into a single movie page.

    As some of you might have noticed, we are finally embracing the future. We have added a responsive layout for our mobile users making life a little easier when it comes to surfing the website from your smart phone.  We hope you enjoy this.
